Make Typography Responsive

Viewport units are relative to the viewport dimensions (width or height) of a device, and percentages are relative to the size of the parent container element.

The four different viewport units are: vw (viewport width): 10vw would be 10% of the viewport's width. vh (viewport height): 3vh would be 3% of the viewport's height. vmin (viewport minimum): 70vmin would be 70% of the viewport's smaller dimension (height or width). vmax (viewport maximum): 100vmax would be 100% of the viewport's bigger dimension (height or width).
